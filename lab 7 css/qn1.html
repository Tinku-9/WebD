VIT (Vellore Institute of Technology) is a prestigious private university located in Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India. Known for its world-class infrastructure and academic excellence, it offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various fields. VIT has earned a global reputation for its research initiatives, innovation, and industry collaborations. The university consistently ranks among the top institutions in India for engineering and technology education. VIT's unique features include a highly competitive entrance exam (VITEEE) and a diverse student body.
